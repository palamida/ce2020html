One-day boat trips for groups

If you are visiting the island of Krk in a small or large group, we offer you numerous ideas for unforgettable experiences! Regardless of whether you are attending a team building event and you are looking for an educational and exciting itinerary, or if you want to have an unforgettable day with family and friends at sea, or if you are celebrating a special occasion, we are at your disposal for creating trips according to your needs and wishes. High-quality ships, a second generation tradition in sailing and an experienced crew guarantee an unforgettable experience. Below you will find several sample proposals and based on your inquiries, we can create a unique trip just for you!

The islet of Košljun with its 4 museums and a Franciscan monastery is the favourite excursion destination of the island of Krk and the Kvarner region. It is located only a stunning 15-minute boat trip away from the Punat quayside.

Mini panorama by boat

Discover the most beautiful maritime zone of the North Adriatic: the Punat bay with Marina Punat and Košljun, the beautiful beaches and the surrounding islands make the boat trip an indispensable experience for all vacations on the island of Krk!

Punat - Krk + mini cruise

An unforgettable boat trip on Kvarneriƒá: Whether you are sailing from Krk to Punat or vice versa, whether you are cruising along the islands of Kormati or Plavnik, or swimming on one of the white beaches, the boat trip will reveal to you landscapes full of surprises and attractions!

The islands of Goli otok and Grgur

Would you like to see what island prisons looked like? The islets of Goli otok and Grgur are an appealing destination for a one-day boat trip from Punat or Krk. Along with a spectacular scenery, swimming in a beautiful sea and a walk, we organise a sightseeing of former infamous prisons.

Rab

From island to island - meet the beautiful and greenest island of the Adriatic: the island of Rab! It is only a 2-hour boat trip away from the island of Krk and it is a destination of groups that choose the island of Krk for a whole-day boat trip.

Daily Fishing & Swimming Tours

Would you like to spend a day to remember at sea? Here's a suggestion: become a fisherman for a day, enjoy learning fishing skills and try the freshest possible - freshly caught fish. Learn how to make fisherman's knots, the basics of sailing and how to fish with a trawl net. Go swimming, sunbathe and enjoy the landscapes. It will be an unforgettable day at sea!

Multiday group tours

An unforgettable experience for your family, friends or for team building: something different and new: Try sailing among islands, go swimming in white hidden bays with a turquoise sea and taste freshly caught fish. Later in the evening, anchor in one of the beautiful islands towns and spend the night in high-quality accommodation. With a comfortable ship and an experienced and pleasant crew, we'll take you on multiday cruises!
